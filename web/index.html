Phonikud

Hebrew Grapheme-to-Phoneme Conversion
for Real-Time Text-to-Speech

Yakov Kolani1 Maxim Melichov2 Cobi Calev1 Morris Alper3
Independent Researcher1 Reichman University2 Tel Aviv University3
Paper Code Demo arXiv

Abstract

Hebrew text-to-speech faces unique challenges due to missing vowel markings and variable stress patterns. We present Phonikud, an efficient, open-source grapheme-to-phoneme system that delivers precise IPA transcriptions with low latency suitable for real-time applications.

Alongside Phonikud, we introduce ILSpeech, a meticulously annotated Hebrew speech corpus with expert IPA labels, designed as a standard benchmark for G2P and TTS development.

Experimental results show that Phonikud significantly improves Hebrew TTS accuracy and speed compared to existing approaches.
