Paper Code Demo arXiv

Abstract

Real-time Hebrew text-to-speech is challenging due to complex orthography and missing stress information. We introduce Phonikud, a lightweight open-source grapheme-to-phoneme system producing accurate IPA transcriptions with minimal latency.

We also release ILSpeech, a new Hebrew speech dataset with expert IPA annotations, providing a benchmark for G2P and TTS training.

Phonikud outperforms previous methods, improving Hebrew TTS quality.
